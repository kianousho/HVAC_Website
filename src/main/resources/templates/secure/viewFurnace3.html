About the Book

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a personal finance classic that challenges conventional wisdom about money. In this book, Robert T. Kiyosaki shares the lessons he learned from his "rich dad" and "poor dad," offering insights into building wealth, investing, and achieving financial independence.

Key Concepts

  • The importance of financial education
  • Assets vs. Liabilities
  • The role of entrepreneurship in building wealth
  • Investing in real estate and other income-generating assets
